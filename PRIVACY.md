# Consolation App - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 14, 2026
 - First publish

## Summary

Consolation does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** Consolation does not:

- Collect personal information
- Transmit data over the internet
- Use analytics or tracking
- Communicate with any external servers

The app has no network entitlements and cannot make or accept network connections.

## Data Storage

All snippets and app data are stored locally on your Mac. Nothing leaves your device.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally, but will never snoop on you or collect analytics. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.
